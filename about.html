Our Core Values

The foundation of everything we do at Chandani Secondary School

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that combines academic excellence with character development, preparing students to meet global challenges while remaining rooted in Nepali values and traditions.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Lalitpur's premier institution for holistic education, developing innovative thinkers and ethical leaders who will shape a better future for our nation and the world.

Our Values

  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Respect for diversity
  • Commitment to excellence
  • Social responsibility

Our Promise

Every student who graduates from Chandani Secondary School will be equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in higher education and life, while maintaining a strong connection to their cultural heritage.

Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings to becoming an educational leader in Lalitpur

1989 - Foundation

Established with just 29 students in a small building in Lalitpur, Chandani Secondary School began its journey with a vision to transform education in Nepal.

2004 - First Recognition

Primary level recogntion upto grade 1.

2008 - Higher Level Education

First batch of SLC appearing student.

2015 - School Expansion

Moved to our current green school in Lalitpur, featuring modern classrooms, science labs, and sports facilities to accommodate growing demand.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning infrastructure, becoming a pioneer in blended education in Nepal during the pandemic challenges.

हाम्रो प्रबन्ध निर्देशकबाट सन्देश

आदरणीय अभिभावकज्यूहरू, प्रिय विद्यार्थीहरू र शिक्षकज्यूहरू,

चाँदनी अंग्रेजी माध्यमिक विद्यालयले आफ्नो स्थापना देखिनै गुणवत्तायुक्त शिक्षा, आधुनिक शिक्षण पद्धति र विद्यार्थी–केन्द्रित सिकाइ प्रक्रियामार्फत समग्र विकासमा केन्द्रित रहँदै आएको छ। र यस यात्रामा तपाईंहरूको निरन्तर सहयोग, विश्वास र समर्पणको लागि म तपाईंहरूलाई धन्यवाद दिन चाहन्छु।

विद्यार्थीहरूका बहुआयामिक विकासका लागि हामीले शिक्षासँगै सह–पाठ्यक्रम गतिविधिहरू, प्रविधिको प्रयोग, र नैतिक शिक्षामा पनि जोड दिइरहेका छौं। हामी चाहन्छौं कि हाम्रा विद्यार्थीहरू केवल पाठ्यपुस्तकको ज्ञानमा मात्र सिमित नहोस्, बरु उनीहरू आत्मनिर्भर, जिम्मेवार र समाजप्रति संवेदनशील नागरिक बनून्।

अन्त्यमा, म सम्पूर्ण अभिभावकज्यूहरूलाई आफ्ना बालबालिकाको शिक्षामा सक्रिय सहभागी हुन अनुरोध गर्दछु। अभिभावक, शिक्षक र विद्यार्थीबीचको सशक्त समन्वयमार्फत नै शिक्षाको गुणस्तर उकास्न सकिन्छ भन्ने हाम्रो दृढ विश्वास छ।

तपाईंहरूको विश्वास, सहयोग र साथप्रति हामी सधैँ आभारी छौँ।
