Public Transport Access

  • Local buses to Shankhamul stop at Shankhamul Bridge (5 mins walk)
  • Microbuses from Ratna Park terminate at Shankhamul Bus Stop
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services available across Lalitpur

Parking Information

  • Limited parking available on campus for visitors
  • Paid parking available at Kick Futsal Parking Lot (1 min walk)

Visiting Guidelines

For the safety and security of our students, all visitors must:

  • Register at the security gate
  • Wear the provided visitor badge at all times
  • Follow all school policies while on campus

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions we receive

Our regular school hours are Sunday to Thursday from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM. On Fridays, school ends at 1:00 PM for religious observances. The administrative office is open Sunday to Thursday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM and on Fridays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

We welcome prospective families to visit our campus. Tours are conducted Monday to Thursday between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Please call our admission office at +977-1-5912488 or email info@chandani.edu.np to schedule your visit. Tours typically last about an hour and include visits to classrooms, facilities, and a meeting with admission staff.

Admission requires the following documents: completed application form, two recent passport-sized photographs, birth certificate (original and photocopy), previous school report cards, transfer certificate (if applicable), character certificate from previous school, and citizenship copies of parents/guardians. Additional documents may be required for specific circumstances.

Our fee structure varies by grade level and includes tuition fee, development fee, and other applicable charges. We offer discounts for siblings and early payment. For detailed information about the current academic year's fees, please contact our accounts department at info@chandani.edu.np or call +977-1-5912488.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students. Financial assistance is also available for families with demonstrated need. Applications for financial aid are accepted from April 1 to May 15 each year. All financial aid decisions are made confidentially by our scholarship committee. Please email info@chandani.edu.np for application details and requirements.
